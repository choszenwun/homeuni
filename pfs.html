PFS Recovery Protocol

PHARMACEUTICAL INJURY → ACTIVE RECOVERY CONSCIOUSNESS + LIFESTYLE + NEUROGENESIS + COMPOUNDS FREE

Full recovery framework for post-finasteride syndrome. Consciousness-first approach combined with lifestyle protocols, neurogenesis, and targeted supplementation. Everything I used. Nothing held back.

Premise

How This Works

No complete pharmaceutical fix exists for PFS. Some people will sell you $10,000/month steroid protocols. This isn't that.

This is a natural-first system that maximises what your body can do on its own before introducing exogenous compounds. Four pillars: consciousness work, lifestyle protocols, neurogenesis, and exogenous intervention as a final layer.

Consciousness comes first because your mind constructs your physiological reality. The placebo effect is a measurable neurological event. We're using it alongside everything else.

Prerequisite

Study the Whitepill consciousness course on this site first. That's the theoretical framework. This protocol is applied. The consciousness work is load-bearing, not optional.

Sequence

Natural First, Exogenous Last

Know your natural recovery ceiling before adding variables. If you start TRT in month one, you'll never know what your body could have done alone. Get as far as you can with consciousness, lifestyle, and neurogenesis. Then fill gaps with medical tools.

Disclaimer

Not medical advice. Not a doctor. Protocols built from research and personal experimentation. Do your own research. Start low. Track everything. Crisis support: Lifeline 13 11 14 (AU).

Framework

Eight Principles

01 — Reframe IdentityYou are recovering, not sick. Accept the injury happened — denial keeps you stuck. But don't identify as a victim — that locks you into the condition. "I am recovering" is a direction. "I am sick" is a prison.
02 — Study ConsciousnessUnderstand why mind over matter works mechanistically. Your brain constructs your experience of reality. The placebo effect generates measurable physiological change from belief and intention. Learn how (Whitepill course), then deploy it.
03 — Control Reality InputsCut anyone who denies your experience. Stop doom-scrolling PFS forums once you've gathered what you need. Every input you allow sets a standard in your consciousness.
04 — Rest AggressivelyChemical injury. Your body needs recovery time. Reduce work obligations. Use government benefits if needed. When I quit my job and allowed the full crash, my body collapsed — then began recovering. It was waiting for the space.
05 — Clean LifestyleClean eating, total sobriety, consistent exercise. Non-negotiable. Your body is repairing damaged systems. Don't make it fight inflammation and neurotoxicity simultaneously.
06 — Semen RetentionMinimum 90 days. Builds energetic charge, increases clarity. Whether the mechanism is metaphysical, hormonal, or placebo doesn't matter — results are consistent.
07 — Neurogenesis ProtocolsMedicinal mushrooms for neural pathway repair. Psilocybin for neuroplasticity. Targeted repair tools. Introduce only when stable.
08 — Exogenous LastTRT and hormonal interventions after natural recovery is maximised. Know your natural ceiling first.
Key Mechanic

The Identity Paradox

Accept you were injured — process the grief, the rage, all of it. Denial keeps you stuck. But don't become the injury. "PFS sufferer" is an identity that generates more suffering. "Person recovering from pharmaceutical poisoning" is a trajectory. Every identity generates a frequency. That frequency shapes what you manifest.

Daily Systems

Non-Negotiables

01
Sleep

Highest-impact variable. Neurological repair happens during sleep.

  • Total darkness — blackout curtains or eye mask
  • Fixed sleep/wake times
  • No screens in bed — read instead
  • Cool room, warm blankets
  • Magnesium glycinate PM
  • Can't sleep: get up, do something boring, return when tired
02
Exercise

Daily movement. Most reliable dopamine and hormone boost available.

  • Walking — minimum 30 minutes, morning sunlight
  • Resistance training — compound movements, progressive overload, 3-5x/week
  • Don't overtrain — body is already under systemic stress
03
Nutrition

Very clean. Best raw materials for repair.

  • Whole foods — meat, fish, eggs, vegetables, fruit, nuts, seeds
  • Anti-inflammatory — omega-3s, turmeric, leafy greens
  • Adequate protein
  • More water than you think
04
Sobriety

Total. No alcohol, no recreational drugs. Alcohol is neurotoxic. Your brain is injured. Don't poison it further.

05
Semen Retention

Minimum 90 days. Builds charge, increases clarity. Studied in the consciousness framework.

06
Meditation

10-20 minutes daily. Trains discipline for identity reframing. Supports neurological repair through cortisol management. Visualise recovery. Practise directing consciousness.

07
Nervous System Regulation

Chronic dysregulation is likely. Downregulate to create repair conditions.

  • Breathwork — box breathing, physiological sighs
  • Cold exposure — cold showers, cold plunge
  • Vagus nerve — humming, gargling, cold water on face
  • Nature — sun on skin, barefoot if accessible

Structure

Two Phases

Most protocols only cover Phase 1. Phase 1 works partly by routing around the hardest issues. Eventually you come back for what you avoided.

Phase 1
Stabilise & Rebuild

Bulk of this protocol. Consciousness work, clean lifestyle, sobriety, retention, removing toxic inputs, neurogenesis, supplementation. Optimal conditions for neurological repair.

Gets you from rock bottom to functional and further. Also constructs a life where the injury isn't tested under real conditions. Strategic, not weak. But it has a ceiling.

Phase 2
Reintegration

Reintroduce what you've avoided: intimacy, ejaculation, social connection, coming off monk mode. Test recovery in real conditions.

Key unknowns: Can you maintain recovery while ejaculating regularly? Does intimacy trigger crashes? How much remaining dysfunction is physical vs. psychological shutdown? You won't know until you test. Test carefully.

Limitations

Ceiling

Some damage may have a ceiling for natural recovery. If you plateau despite doing everything right, that tells you where exogenous intervention is needed. You can only get that data by doing the natural work first.

Counterproductive

What to Avoid

If the core issue is understimulation and anhedonia, these make it worse. You're not overstimulated. Don't sedate what's already numb.

L-TheanineGABAergic. Adds dampening when you need activation.
AshwagandhaBlunts emotional range, flattens affect. Counterproductive for anhedonia.
High-Dose Magnesium During the DaySedating. PM only.
Anything Marketed as "Calm"If it's GABAergic or sedating, default to caution. You need activation.
PFS Forum Doom ScrollingGather what you need, get out. Past that you're programming your perception filter for hopelessness.
People Who Deny ItNot unhelpful — actively destabilising. Stop discussing it with them.
